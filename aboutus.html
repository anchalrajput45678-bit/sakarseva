About Sakar Sewa

Every blessed human has a kind side and wants to help the less fortunate-but often doesn't know where to start or how to find the right path. The SS Foundation, established in 2025, is a registered non-profit, non-governmental organization dedicated to community development, child welfare, education, and healthcare initiatives.

We believe that education is both a means and an end to a better life: a means because it empowers individuals to earn their livelihood, and an end because it raises awareness about a wide range of social issues. As an environment-friendly, socio-economic, and cultural development organization, we are committed to improving the lives and livelihoods of disadvantaged people across India. Our inclusive efforts span across multiple sectors including education, healthcare, elderly care, social and economic empowerment, food and livelihood security, and water and sanitation.
