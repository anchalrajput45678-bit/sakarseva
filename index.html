Overview

Sakar Seva Foundation

Sakar Seva Foundation established in 2025, is a registered non-profit, non-governmental organization dedicated to community development, child welfare, education, and healthcare initiatives. We believe that education is both a means and an end to a better life: a means because it empowers individuals to earn their livelihood, and an end because it raises awareness about a wide range of social issues.
